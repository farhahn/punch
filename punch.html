About Us

Gram Panchayat Examplevila is a vibrant rural community located in the heart of India. Established in 1950, our panchayat serves over 2,000 residents, focusing on sustainable development, education, health, and agriculture. We strive to empower our villagers through various government schemes and community initiatives, ensuring inclusive growth and prosperity for all.

News/Updates

New Water Supply Project Launched

Date: August 15, 2025

We are excited to announce the launch of a new water supply project that will provide clean drinking water to all households in Examplevila. This initiative is funded by the state government and aims to improve health and hygiene in our community.

Community Health Camp Organized

Date: July 20, 2025

A free health camp was organized in collaboration with local NGOs, where over 500 villagers received medical check-ups and vaccinations. We thank all participants and volunteers for making it a success.
